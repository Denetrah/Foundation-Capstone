Salt & Straw's Vegetable Ice Cream Flavors Are Packed With Carrots, Spinach, And Corn

by Danielle Harling August 18, 2022

When your parents told you to eat your veggies, they probably never imagined a day when eating vegetables would consist of devouring a bowl of ice cream. Well, the Portland, Oregon-based ice cream company Salt & Straw just introduced its "Veggies You Crave" line of new flavors, and it's is brimming with carrots, spinach, corn, and more. The veggie-inspired flavors in the new collection are: Carrot Cake Batter with Pralined Hazelnuts: Spinach Cake with Chocolate Tahini Fudge Green Fennel & Maple Charred Corn Curd, Cotija & Tajin Red Chili Curry & Makrut Lime Crispy Rice

Subway Will Give You Free Sandwiches For Life If You Get A Footlong Tattoo How deep is your Subway love?

by Josh Davis August 11, 2022

Subway is going all out for its news Subway Series, a lineup of 12 signature sandwiches. Just a handful of days after Subway gave away close to one million sandwiches, the chain has yet another wild promotion up its sleeveâ€”or in this case, it is a sleeve. On August 27, at Las Vegas' Bad Apple Tattoo, Subway will host a block party where nine Subway superfans will be able to receive a tattoo of the Subway Series logo by two-time Ink Master champion DJ Tambe or a member of his team. Depending on where the sandwich fanatics receive the tattoo and how big it is, they'll be able to receive free Subway sandwiches for a month, a year, or even a lifetime. To get free subs for their entire life, a Subway fan will have to receive a 12" x 12" tattoo of the logo on their sternum or back. A 2" x 2" tattoo on their wrist, bicep, or foot will win free subs for a month, and a 3" x 3" tattoo on their shoulder blade, forearm, or calf will win free subs for a year. If you prefer a not-so-permanent way to show your love for Subway, airbrush tattoos will also be available at the event. Being a true block party, there will be free Subway sandwiches, a DJ, dance floor, and a graffiti artist on hand to create a mural. All the details can be found here.
